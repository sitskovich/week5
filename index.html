Key Vocabulary

Photosynthesis
Process where plants make food using sunlight.
Chlorophyll
Green pigment that captures sunlight.
Roots
Anchor plants & absorb nutrients/water.
Stems
Transport water & nutrients; support plant.
Seeds
Grow into new plants.

🌿 Plant Anatomy

Plants have four main parts: roots, stems, leaves, and flowers.
