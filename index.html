🌼 Plants Have Five Main Parts

🧾 What Do Plant Parts Do?

  • Flower: For reproduction, makes seeds.
  • Fruit: Protects seeds, helps spread them.
  • Seeds: Grow into new plants.
  • Leaf: Makes food using sunlight.
  • Stem: Supports plant, moves water and food.
  • Roots: Take in water/nutrients, hold plant in place.
